Proverbs:

Out of Sight, Out of Mind: When something or someone is not present or visible, it is easily forgotten or no longer thought about.
Sentence: After the vacation, the daily routine seemed easier to follow—out of sight, out of mind, as they say.

The Forbidden Fruit is Always the Sweetest: People often desire what is forbidden or out of reach more than what is easily accessible.
Sentence: Even though her parents warned her not to sneak out, the forbidden fruit was always the sweetest, and she couldn’t resist.
